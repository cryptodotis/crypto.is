The Crypto Project

The Crypto Project is a Non-Profit Organization Designed to Fund Anonymity and Encryption Research, Development, and Support.  It's main goal is to revive the Cypherpunk movement to provide better software, security, and anonymity to individuals worldwide, regardless of nation or state.

Projects

Remailers

The support of various Anonymous Remailers such as Mixmaster, Mixminion and various others. Also the support of new, more advanced and secure implementations.

Anonymity

The support of various Anonimizers such as Tor, I2P, and others. The Crypto Project will advance the research into these protocols as well as create and develop new ones.

Chat

The Crypto Project will research and develop tools such as Off-the-Record (OTR) Messaging.

Currency

The Crypto Project will provide analysis and evaluations into protocols such as Bitcoin and the development of new decentralized, secure, digital currencies.

Mobile

The Crypto Project looks to fund development of mobile applications related to anonymity, encryption, and communication (similar to The Guardian Project and Whisper Systems.

Storage

The Crypto Project is looking to support tools like GNU Privacy Guard to provide secure storage encryption.

Donate and Contribute

The Crypto Project is always looking for developers to contribute, researchers to give their time, and individuals who can run servers to support Crypto.is sponsored projects. If you cannot devote time to the project, The Crypto Project is always welcoming donations to support the projects developers, researchers, and to help pay for and keep anonymity servers running. Crypto.is accepts donations via credit card, Paypal, or Bitcoin.
