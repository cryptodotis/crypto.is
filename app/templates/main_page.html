The Crypto.is Project

Crypto.is is a Non-Profit Organization Designed to Fund Anonymity and Encryption Research, Development, and Support.  It's main goal is to revive the Cypherpunk movement to provide better software, security, and anonymity to individuals worldwide, regardless of nation or state.
