The Crypto Project

The Crypto Project is an organization designed to assist and encourage anonymity and encryption research, development, and use.  As part of this goal we seek to revive the Cypherpunk movement to provide better software, security, and anonymity to individuals worldwide, regardless of nation or state.

Areas

Remailers

The support of various Anonymous Remailers such as Mixmaster, Mixminion and others. The support and development of new, more advanced and secure implementations.

Anonymity

The support of various Anonimizers such as Tor, I2P, and others.

Chat

Development and maintenance of tools such as Off-the-Record (OTR) Messaging.

E-Mail

Encourage adoption of secure e-mail protocols and software - like S/MIME and Enigmail.

Mobile

Encourage development of mobile applications related to anonymity, encryption, and communication - both whole-phone solutions such as The Guardian Project and Whisper Systems and individual applications.

Storage

Support and Audit of tools like GNU Privacy Guard and Truecrypt to provide secure storage encryption.

Currency

Encourage analysis and evaluation of protocols such as Bitcoin and other decentralized, secure, digital currencies.

Protocols

Assist in the development and adoption of future secure protocols, such as DANE and HSTS.

Join In

The Crypto Project is always looking for developers to contribute, researchers to give their time, and individuals who can run servers. Monetary donations will be accepted sparingly for the time being.
