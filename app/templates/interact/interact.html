Interact

There are many ways to interact and get involved with the various anonymity projects out there. To get involved with Crypto.is:

Donate Your Time

Are you a developer? Can you write documentation? Crypto.is is looking for anyone that can contribute to a project in anyway. Check out Contributing Time for more information.

Fund Anonymity Servers

To donate funds to Crypto.is, learn what we use the money for, and to find information about donating to other worthy projects, check out Contributing Money

Contribute Servers, Shares and Bandwidth

One of the best ways to help out anonymity projects is to setup and run a server yourself. But if the thought of that intimidates you, we'll help you out, either hands on or off. Check out Contributing Goods to find out how you can put spare servers or bandwidth to good use.
